Escape in the Basement

A sandbox survival story about resourcefulness, rebuilding, and the small freedoms we cling to when the world has run out of easy choices. Built to play how you want — offline, modded, or just exploring.

About the Project

Escape in the Basement is a 3D exploration and survival game built around the idea of freedom within decay. No single path, no fixed outcome — just the player, the world, and what they make of it.

  • Customizable systems — switch combat, vehicles, or NPCs on or off
  • Procedural loot and environmental storytelling
  • Offline‑first design with mod support and local saves
